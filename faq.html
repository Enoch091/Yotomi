FAQ

Ordering & Delivery

How do I place an order?
›
Visit the menu, select your preferred soup, add to cart, and proceed to checkout.
What time do deliveries happen?
›
Deliveries are made on Sundays & Wednesdays between 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM.
Can I cancel or change my order after they are sent?
›
No, once an order is placed, it cannot be modified or canceled.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?
›
Payments are made via bank transfer only, with details provided at checkout.
Do you accept cash on delivery?
›
No, we only accept pre-payment via bank transfer.

Pickup & Delivery

Where is the delivery location?
›
Location to be filled in the checkout page during order placement.
